Welcome to Faraday Finder

Faraday Finder intends to conglomerate a vast number of research laboratories form university, public and industrial iinstitutions. Searchable laboratory databases enable time efficiency improvements for prospective students at all levels of education lookiing for a lab. Additionally, Faraday Finder solidifies individual and lab identity while enhancing inter and intralaboratorial communication and collaboration on a web platform
